What if 'school' evolved?

'School' still has many limitations...

* Most institutions of learning around the world are still limited in many ways.
- They are limited in their impact.
- They are limited in their reach.
- They are limited in their flexibility.
- They are limited in their efficiency.


* Most students around the world are still limited in many ways.
- They are limited in their choice.
- They are limited in their time.
- They are limited in their location.
- They are limited in their finances.

But what if 'school' could evolve to address these limitations?

* What if institutions of learning could just focus on their main objective, providing education in the best possible way for anyone, anywhere?
* What if teachers could just focus on their main objective, providing knowledge in the best possible way for anyone, anywhere?
* What if students could just focus on their main objective, obtaining knowledge in their own way and at their own pace, from anywhere?


Anyone, anywhere in the world could start an institution of learning and institutions of learning should...

* Be able to achieve their main purpose of providing access to the best education in the most efficient way to anyone, anywhere in the world and in the cheapest way possible.
* Be able to have easy access to the best teaching talent around the world and leverage their gifts to provide knowledge.
* Be able to accurately tell how their enrolled students are faring.


Anyone, anywhere in the world could be a phenomenal teacher and teachers should...

* Be able to have the freedom to teach from wherever in the world they are.
* Be able to have the freedom to teach in whichever school in the world they want.
* Be able to have the freedom to easily teach multiple schools located in different parts of the world, if they wish.


Anyone, anywhere in the world could learn anything and learners should...

* Be able to enroll in any school in the world without moving from where they are.
* Be able to have the freedom to learn from wherever they are and at their own pace.
* Be able to take charge of their education and determine which kinds of knowledge they should consume and from which schools in the world they should consume it from.

Through scholar, school could evolve...

* Anyone in the world can create an institutions of learning without putting up a physical building.
* Any institutions of learning can have easy access to the best teaching talent in the entire world.
* Any institutions of learning can have access to any student accross the globe and thus can scale their impact. They do not have to be limited to a physical space.

* Anyone with the gift of teaching a subject can easily become a teacher without giving up what they may be currently doing.
* Teachers can easily teach in multiple schools located anywhere in the world at the same time.

* Learners can have access to education from any institution of learning from anywhere in the world.
* Learners can learn at their own pace.
* Learners can easily take charge of their education if they want to.
